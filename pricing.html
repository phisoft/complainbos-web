Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will be prorated and take effect immediately.

Is there a setup fee?

No, there are no setup fees for any of our plans. The Free Trial and Standard plans can be activated instantly.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits?

Yes, we offer special pricing for non-profit organizations and educational institutions. Contact our sales team for details.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we use enterprise-grade security with encryption, regular backups, and comply with data protection regulations. Your data is stored securely in our SOC2-compliant data centers.
